Privacy Policy

At Image Tools Studio, your privacy is our top priority. Our platform is designed to be "Privacy by Design."

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any of the images you process on our website. All image processing (compression and resizing) happens locally in your browser using client-side JavaScript.

No Server Uploads

Your files never leave your computer. We do not have a backend server that receives your image data. This ensures that your personal or professional photos remain strictly on your device.

Cookies

We use local storage only to remember your theme preference (Dark/Light mode). We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics that identify you personally.
